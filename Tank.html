A Tank's job is to have the enemy team focus their spells (especially disables) and attacks on them so their teammates can do what they need to do without getting utterly owned.
Thus in team-battles, a Tank is often the one who is given the responsibility to initiate a favorable fight by going in head-first and disabling/singling out high priority, vulnerable targets.
Knowing how and when to initiate is at the very core essence of being a Tank.
