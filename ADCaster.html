AD Casters rely more on their skill-set for damage as opposed to an AD carry which rely on auto-attacks. One key requirement is for their skills to scale well with Additionally in terms of play style most AD casters will have and use their gap closers to initiate on an enemy whereas an AD carry generally reserves them for kiting AD casters generally do not require attack speed in their build, but since their positioning is generally more dangerous than that of an AD carry survivability might become a problem. In some cases early defensive items and careful positioning might be required if you find yourself unable to finish your burst.

  • Wukong, The Monkey King. His abilities are:
    Crushing Blow: Wukong's next attack within 5 seconds gains range, deals additional physical damage and reduces his target's armor.

    Decoy: Wukong enters stealth and leaves behind a decoy of himself which, vanishes and deals magic damage to all nearby enemies.

    Nimbus Strike: Wukong dashes on a cloud to the target enemy and dealing physical damage to each enemy struck.

    Cyclone: ( ult ) Wukong spins around for up to 4 seconds, losing the ability to use abilities and basic attacks but dealing physical damage to all nearby enemies every second.
  • Zed, Master Of Shadows. His abilities are:
    Razor Shuriken: Zed throws his spinning blades forward, dealing physical damage to the first enemy they pass through.

    Living Shadow: Zed's shadow dashes forward, remaining in place. Reactivating Living Shadow causes Zed to blink to its location, swapping places with it.

    Shadow Slash: Zed spins his blades, dealing physical damage to nearby enemies.

    Death Mark: ( ult ) Zed becomes untargetable for 0.75 seconds and dashes behind the target enemy champion.
  • Pantheon, The Artisian Of War. His abilities are:
    Spear Shot: Pantheon hurls his spear at the target enemy, dealing them physical damage.

    Aegis Of Zeonia: Pantheon dashes to the target enemy, dealing them magic damage.

    Heartseeker Strike: Pantheon Critically strikes all enemies below 15% of their maximum health with his basic attacks and Spear Shot.

    Grand Skyfall: ( ult ) Pantheon begins channeling for 2 seconds and, upon completing the channel, leaps into the air and becomes untargetable.
  • Riven, The Exile. Her abilities are:
    Ki Burst: Riven's sword emits a burst of runic energy that shocks nearby enemies, dealing physical damage and stunning them.

    Valor: Riven dashes towards the cursor and gains a shield that absorbs incoming damage for 1.5 seconds.

    Blade Of The Exile: Riven reforms her sword for 15 seconds, gaining 20% AD as bonus attack damage, increasing her attack range by 75 for a total of 200.

    Wind Slash: ( ult ) Riven unleashes a wave of energy in a cone, dealing physical damage to all enemies hit.
