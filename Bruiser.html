A Bruiser is a tank and an attack damage mix. Bruisers generally also have pretty strong lane sustain that allow
them to farm. They're steady and reliable DPS for your team, not high or accurate enough to be a classic carry but enough of a threat to warrant focusing.
They can also dish out TONS OF DAMAGE (As Phreak would say), but not enough to count as a true Carry.

A couple of Bruiser Champions are:
Garen
Darius
Irelia
Renekton

  • Garen, The Might Of Demacia. His abilities are:
    Decisive Strike: Garen cleanses himself of all slows affecting him and gains bonus movement speed.

    Courage: Garen permanently gains bonus armor and magic resistance every time he kills an enemy.

    Judgement: Garen rapidly spins his sword around his body, losing the ability to use basic attacks.

    Demacian Justice: ( ult ) The enemy with the most kills in the last 5 minutes is marked as the Villain, causing Garen's basic attacks to do bonus damage.
  • Darius, The Hand Of Noxus. His abilities are:
    Decimate: Darius hefts his axe, then swings it around himself, dealing physical damage to nearby enemies.

    Crippling Strike: Darius's next basic attack deals 40% AD bonus physical damage and slows his target for 1 second.

    Apprehend: After a brief delay, Darius pulls in all enemies in front of him, slowing them.

    Noxian Guillotine: ( ult ) Darius dashes to the target enemy champion and attempts to execute them, dealing true damage.
  • Irelia, The Will Of The Blades. Her abilities are:
    BladeSurge: Irelia dashes to the target enemy, dealing physical damage and applying on-hit effects.

    Hiten Style: relia's basic attacks deal bonus true damage.

    Equilibrium Strike: Irelia pierces the target enemy, dealing them magic damage and slowing them.

    Transcendent Blades: ( ult ) Irelia summons 4 spirit blades and fires one in a line towards the cursor, dealing physical damage to all enemies it passes through.
  • Renekton, The Butcher Of The Sands. His abilities are:
    Cull The Meek: Renekton deals physical damage to all nearby enemies, gaining fury for each enemy non-champion hit and fury for each enemy champion hit.

    Ruthless Predator: Renekton's next basic attack gains 50 bonus range and strikes his target twice.

    Slice: Renekton dashes in the target direction, dealing physical damage to all enemies he passes through.

    Dominus: ( ult ) Renekton empowers himself for 15 seconds, gaining bonus health, increased size and 50 bonus range on his basic attacks and Cull the Meek.
